# PingPongDifficulty
A Civ 6 mod to change the difficulty settings for our ongoing multiplayer games

## Difficulty Modifiers
| Difficulty  | Science / Culture / Faith Bonus | Production / Gold Bonus |  Barbarian Camp Gold |
| ------------- | ------------- | ------------- | ------------- |
| Settler | +24%  | +20% | 30 |
| Chieftain | +16%  | +2% | 30 |
| Warlord | +8%  | +1% | 30 |
| Prince | +0%  | +0%  | 30 |
| King | -8%  | -1%  | 30 |
| Emperor | -16%  | -2%  | 30 |
| Immortal | -24%  | -4%  | 30 |
| Deity | -32%  | -8%  | 30 |
